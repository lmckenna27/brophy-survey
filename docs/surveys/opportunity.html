Privllege Index Survey

Please take a few minutes to thoughtfully complete this survey. It is designed to help you reflect on how different factors - such as family background, education, identity, and access to resources - can shape your experiences and opportunities.

  • There are no right or wrong answers.
  • Be honest and answer based on your own life.
  • Take your time and respond to each statement carefully. The goal is self-reflection, not judgment.
  • Your responses are confidential and will only be used to promote understanding and empathy within our school community.
